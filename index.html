Effortless editing and consistency

SnapHive enables users to add annotations, shapes, and text to screenshots without altering the original image. When application interfaces change, simply update the screenshot while keeping your annotations intact. This flexibility ensures quick updates and consistent documentation, saving valuable time and effort.

SnapHive's tagging feature simplifies how you organize and find your screenshots. By tagging each screenshot, you can effortlessly categorize and search your visual documentation. This is ideal for quickly navigating large projects or pinpointing specific features for updates, ensuring your documentation process is both time-efficient and straightforward.

Enhance collaboration by enabling teams to work on documentation together, supported by a robust versioning system. This ensures easy updates and access, keeping your project’s documentation accurate and unified.

Key features

Annotation flexibility

Enhance your visual content with ease using our intuitive editing tools that allow for real-time updates and perfect placement — no rework necessary.

  • Dynamic annotations: Add and adjust shapes, arrows, and text without modifying the base image.
  • Consistent styling: Maintain uniformity in design with standardized colors and formats.
  • Editable layers: Revisit and edit annotations at any time for up-to-date documentation.
  • Flexible annotations: Adjust and export to PNG or JPEG while keeping elements editable for future changes.

Streamlined tagging system

Easily categorize and locate your screenshots with our intuitive tagging feature, designed to keep your screenshots organized and accessible.

  • Streamlined organization: Categorize screenshots by language, section, or feature for easy organization and retrieval.
  • Quick access: Use tag-based search and filtering for fast, precise retrieval of screenshots.
  • Update efficiency: Identify and update impacted screenshots with tags, keeping documentation accurate.

Versioning

Ensure your screenshots stay current and traceable with SnapHive's advanced versioning capabilities.

  • Track changes: View and manage each screenshot's history to monitor adjustments over time.
  • Seamless updates: Safely update screenshots while retaining access to earlier versions for potential rollback.
  • Change accountability: Monitor who made specific changes, adding transparency to your documentation workflow.

Seamless integration

Elevate your workflow with SnapHive's seamless integration capabilities.

  • Platform compatibility: Connect with tools like Zendesk and Confluence, embedding screenshots directly into your workflow.
  • Effortless sharing: Generate shareable links to annotated screenshots for easy access across teams and clients.
  • Streamlined documentation: Embed screenshots into documentation platforms seamlessly, ensuring resources stay synchronized and current.
